About

It is not about us, it is about technology that brings tangible benefits for investors and environment.

We are polish family owned company, that provides economically reliable solutions for plastic waste accommodation, based on over 25 years of experience in pyrolytic conversion of wastes into the fuel oils.

POLAND

In the 90’s of the last century, thanks to the pyrolytic oil excise tax waiver, Poland became the nation the most dynamically developing pyrolysis of the plastic waste, also known as plastic-waste-to-fuel technology. More than 20 industrial size pyrolytic reactors have been built at the country the size of California. In 2005 pyrolytic fuel and crude oil fuel taxes have been aligned and pyrolytic oil production was financially viable only with the use of the best technologies. The rapid development of technology in the last decade of the 20th century was strong enough to establish the mature technology of pyrolysis of plastic waste, that lets investors obtain the fuel oils, synthetic diesel and other petrochemical products in commercial scale.
